{{How are ARE dates calculated? ARE dates arise when a set number of days have passed since the most recent Home Office or Tribunal decision has been made regarding a particular case. The number of days is determined by the decision type and the stage of the appeal. The ARE date can’t fall on a weekend or on an {{#t}}exclusion date{{/t}}, so will fall on the next suitable date.
